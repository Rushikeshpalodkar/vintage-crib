How does billing work?
Currently, subscriptions are managed by our admin team. Contact us to upgrade your plan and we'll handle the billing process for you.
Can I change my plan anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.
What platforms can I cross-post to?
Depending on your plan, you can post to eBay, Poshmark, Depop, Mercari, and our own Vintage Crib marketplace. Higher tiers unlock more platforms.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! Every account starts with our Free tier so you can explore the platform. You can list up to 5 items and use our basic features at no cost.
What kind of support do you offer?
Support varies by tier: Free users get community support, Starter gets email support, Pro gets priority email support, and Premium gets priority phone and email support.
